Deploy Form Builder designs

A status-change deploys (publishes) or inactivates a Form

A Form Builder design starts in a design (new) state. It's not published immediately, so it's Form (Runner) isn't accessible to any user. Publishing the form is just a regular status change on the Odoo statusbar. After publishing users can access and fill-in the Form (Runner).
